# 🏡 Housewarming Day

A simple and thoughtful way to invite people into our new home, bringing together presence confirmation, event planning, and meaningful contributions. ✨🎉

## 🌟 Essence of the Project

This project is a digital space created for our housewarming event, designed to make everything clear, organized, and special for everyone involved.

Instead of scattered messages and manual coordination, everything lives in one place:

* 📅 View the full event schedule for the day
* ✅ Confirm attendance
* 🎁 Contribute to our new home by supporting specific furniture and items
* 💛 Feel part of this new chapter we are building together

---

## 🚀 Tech Overview

* **Frontend:** React (Next.js)
* **CMS:** Strapi (event data, guests, and contribution items)
* **Purpose:** Lightweight, dynamic, and easy to manage all content controlled through the CMS

---

## 💛 Why this exists

Because a home is not built alone.

This project is a way to share this moment with the people who matter most making it easier to celebrate together and contribute to something meaningful. 🏡✨
